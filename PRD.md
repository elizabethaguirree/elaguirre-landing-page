# Product Requirement Document

## 01 — Problem Statement

Elizabeth Aguirre is a Business Analytics & Information Systems and Marketing student at the University of Iowa graduating in May 2026 who is pursuing entry-level roles in business analytics and marketing. While a resume lists experiences and skills, it does not fully demonstrate her ability to translate data into insights, communicate strategy, and connect technical analysis with real business outcomes. This landing page provides a centralized professional presence where recruiters and hiring managers can quickly understand Elizabeth’s skills, projects, and professional direction.

---

## 02 — Target Audience

The primary audience for this landing page is recruiters and hiring managers seeking candidates for entry-level business analytics or marketing roles, particularly within companies located in the Chicago area. These visitors are likely looking for candidates who demonstrate strong analytical skills, business acumen, and the ability to communicate insights clearly.

---

## 03 — Goals & Success Metrics

| Goal | How We Measure It |
|-----|-----|
| Increase visibility to recruiters hiring for analytics and marketing roles | At least 100 unique visits to the landing page within the first 60 days |
| Generate interest from potential employers | Receive 5–10 recruiter messages or LinkedIn connection requests referencing the site |
| Support job search outcomes | Secure 3–5 interviews for entry-level business analytics or marketing roles within 60 days |

## 06 — Assumptions & Constraints

### Assumptions

- A GitHub repository for the project has already been created

- Development time is limited to the timeframe of the course assignment
